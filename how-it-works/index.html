How It Works

Understanding Scrollless

🎯 Step 1: Select Your Apps

Choose the apps where you tend to get stuck and waste time — social media, entertainment, games, or any app that pulls you in for longer than you'd like.

Important: Select all the apps you want to manage for Scrollless to work effectively. This prevents you from simply jumping between apps when one timer ends.

⏱️ Step 2: Set a Timer

Every time you try to open one of your selected apps, you'll be asked to set a timer:

  • Up to 1 hour: Choose how long you want to use the app
  • No time limit until midnight: Use the app freely until the end of the day

Wait Times

Depending on your timer choice, there may be a brief delay before you can access the app:

  • Up to 10 minutes: Instant access — no wait!
  • 11-60 minutes: A scaled delay (longer timers = longer wait, up to ~45 seconds for 1 hour)
  • No time limit: 5-minute delay

During the wait time, ads may be displayed. You cannot leave this screen — if you do, you'll be asked to set a new timer.

✅ Step 3: Use Your Apps

Once you've waited (if applicable) and your timer starts, you can freely use all the apps you've selected — not just the one you opened. Use them however you like until the timer runs out.

🛑 Step 4: Timer Ends — Mandatory Break

When your timer expires, you'll be blocked from all selected apps for 5 minutes. This prevents the classic Instagram → TikTok → YouTube jump that happens when you get bored.

After the 5-minute break, the timer selection screen will appear again when you try to open any of your selected apps.

🔄 Early Reset: Take a Break Anytime

You don't have to wait for the timer to end. If you take a minimum 5-minute break from all selected apps while your timer is still running, the timer will automatically reset.

This rewards you for stepping away voluntarily!

🔧 Troubleshooting

If you experience any issues with Scrollless, restarting your phone will fix most problems. The app's process reboots with your device and typically resolves any glitches.

For persistent issues, visit our troubleshooting page or contact us at scrollless.app@gmail.com.

📋 Summary

  1. Select all apps you want to manage
  2. Set a timer when opening any selected app
  3. Wait (if applicable) while ads play
  4. Use your apps freely until the timer ends
  5. Take a mandatory 5-minute break when the timer expires
  6. Repeat — or take a voluntary 5-minute break to reset early!
