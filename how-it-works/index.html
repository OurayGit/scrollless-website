Wait Times

Why wait times? The delay incentivizes you to choose shorter sessions. You'll only wait longer if you're truly committed to spending more time on these apps. This creates a natural friction that encourages mindful usage.

Here's how wait times work depending on your timer choice:

  • Up to 10 minutes: Instant access ‚Äî no wait!
  • 11-60 minutes: A scaled delay (longer timers = longer wait, up to ~45 seconds for 1 hour)
  • No time limit: 5-minute delay

During the wait time, ads may be displayed. You cannot leave this screen ‚Äî if you do, you'll be asked to set a new timer.
