How It Works

Understand the science behind Scrollless and how it helps you regain control.

02

Set a Timer

Every time you open a selected app, you set a timer. This simple act of conscious decision-making disrupts the mindless loop.

You choose how long to scroll before the break kicks in.

Wait

Smart Incentives

Why wait times? To encourage shorter sessions. If you choose a long timer, you'll have a short wait. This friction helps you decide if scrolling is really worth it right now.

  • Short timer (10m): Instant access.
  • Long timer (1h): Small delay.
03

Use Your Apps

Once the timer starts, enjoy your scrolling guilt-free. You have full access to all your selected apps for the chosen duration. Use this time however you like!

04

Mandatory Break

When time is up, a 5-minute mandatory break begins. Access to selected apps is paused, giving you a moment to disconnect and reset.

↺
Cycle Restarts

After the 5-minute break, the cycle resets. You can then open an app and set a new timer.
