Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scrollless. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Scrollless app ("App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

Scrollless operates with a privacy-first approach. All usage data is stored locally on your device and includes:

  • App usage statistics (time spent, frequency of use)
  • Your configured settings and preferences
  • Timer and limit configurations
  • Focus mode schedules

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Your app usage data or screen time statistics
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Any content from apps you use

3. Permissions Required

Scrollless requires certain Android permissions to function properly:

3.1 Accessibility Services

Used to monitor and control app usage, enable focus mode, and enforce time limits. This permission allows the App to detect which apps are running and take actions based on your configured settings.

3.2 Usage Access

Used to collect app usage statistics that are displayed to you within the App. This data remains on your device.

3.3 Notification Access

Used to display timely reminders, alerts, and notifications about your app usage and time limits.

Important: These permissions are used exclusively for the App's core functionality and not for data collection, advertising, or tracking purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your usage data is used only to display statistics within the App
  • Settings and preferences are used to customize your experience
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No data is used for advertising or marketing

5. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We have no access to your data
  • Data is not transmitted over the internet
  • Data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the App

6. Third-Party Services

Scrollless does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

Scrollless does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App is designed to be used by individuals of all ages, and since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no privacy concerns specific to children.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App
  • Using the reset function within the App settings (if available)

9. Your Rights

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: View your data anytime within the App
  • Deletion: Delete your data anytime through device settings or App uninstallation
  • Control: Manage all settings and preferences within the App

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice within the App if changes are significant

Your continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. International Users

Scrollless is operated from Switzerland. Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@scrollless.ch

Website: scrollless.ch

13. Compliance

Scrollless is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)
  • Other applicable data protection laws

Since we do not collect or process personal data, compliance with these regulations is naturally maintained.
