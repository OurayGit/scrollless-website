Break the doomscrolling cycle

You close Instagram. Open YouTube. Then TikTok. The native screen time tools? Too easy to tap through. Other apps lock you out entirely.

How It Works

Why Scrollless?

Most screen time apps punish you. Scrollless empowers you.

Intentional, Not Restrictive

You choose how long you want to scroll, like making a promise to yourself. No surprise lockouts, no daily caps, no fighting your own phone.

Cross-App Blocking

Close Instagram, open YouTube, switch to TikTok. Sound familiar? Scrollless blocks all your selected apps at once, so the loop stops.

Smart Incentives

Pick a short timer and get instant access. Pick a long one? Brief wait first. This gentle nudge helps you choose mindfully, not automatically.

Mandatory Breaks

Five-minute breaks between sessions give your brain a chance to reset. Long enough to break the habit loop, short enough to not frustrate you.

Science-Inspired

Built on principles from habit formation research, decision fatigue studies, and commitment device theory. Works with your psychology, not against it.

Free to Use

Scrollless is completely free. Supported by minimal, non-intrusive ads. Prefer no ads? Remove them with a one-time purchase.

The story behind Scrollless

I started building Scrollless over two years ago in Singapore because no existing app worked the way I needed. Native screen time tools were too easy to dismiss, and other apps were too rigid with their daily limits.

So I built something different: an app that respects your freedom but keeps you accountable in the moment. Today I'm a Master's student at the University of St. Gallen, working to keep Scrollless free and useful for everyone.
