02 / Why it works

Daily limits fail. Sessions hold.

The free native tools on iPhone and Android only stop you after a whole day of use, and one tap turns them off. Paid apps block better, but they charge you for the part that helps. Scrollless makes you decide how long the moment you open the app, and stops you when that time is up, not after a whole day. Blocking is free.

04 / Why I made it

I built Scrollless because nothing else worked for me. The built-in tools were too easy to ignore, other apps were too rigid, and the good ones hid the useful parts behind a paywall. But most importantly I was not able to decide flexibly on the situation how long I should have access.
