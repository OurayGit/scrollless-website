    Break the doomscrolling cycle

    That empty feeling after an hour of doomscrolling?

    Set custom session timers for your apps. When time's up, take a five-minute break. Simple, flexible, effective.

    Your eyes hurt, your head is buzzing, and you can almost feel the brainrot setting in. Scrollless helps you break that loop.

    Flexible, not restrictive.

    You choose which apps to keep in check and how long each session should last. When the timer ends, all apps take a short five-minute break. It’s the best moment to put your phone away and get back to what matters.

    Unlike most screen time apps that lock you out for fixed hours, Scrollless adapts to your daily use and limits only how long you stay per session.

    What makes it work

    • Custom session timers for the apps you choose
    • Smart five-minute breaks that reset your focus
